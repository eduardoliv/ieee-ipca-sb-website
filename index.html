Who
Are
We?

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional non-profit organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. We are a Student Branch belonging to IEEE , based at the Polytechnic Institute of Cávado and Ave.

In this nucleus, the development of soft and hard skills is carried out through integration in competitions and organization of events such as Workshops or TalkAbouts and, above all, provides entertainment and socializing among different people.

Join
IEEE!

Step 1 - IEEE Registration

Register on the IEEE official website.
Choose the category “Professional and Education Information” and then select “Instituto Politécnico do Cávado e Ave”.

Membership cost: 27$ (24.30€) annual.

Step 2 - Email Us

Send us an email to ieee.est.sb@ipca.pt containing:
1 - your full name
2 - IEEE membership number
3 - IPCA student ID

Step 3 - Wait for confirmation

We will check your information and send you an email with the confirmation. All the details will be included on the email and you’ll have access to our room.
