Who
Are
We?

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional non-profit organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. We are a Student Branch belonging to IEEE , based at the Polytechnic Institute of Cávado and Ave and composed by IPCA Students and members of the IEEE.

Believing that there’s nothing more powerful that getting together a group of students with the objective of developing skills in order to take the control of their own professional level.

Our branch is the place where our student members will be able to explore, learn and practice along with other people with the same goals.

Benefits

of IEEE
Membership

IEEE delivers insight to the industry's most essential technical information, networking opportunities, career development tools, and many other exclusive benefits to its members.

As the technology is always pushing forward, our membership provides you with the resources and opportunities you need to keep track on technology changes and offers the possibility of get involved in standards development.

The network of professionals, experts and advisors that the IEEE have can help shape your career, offer resources to acquire new skills and accelerate your professional development.

One of the most direct benefits of the membership is the fact that you get access to the largest library of electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics technical literature as well as the latest technology trends.

As an association you are able to collaborate with IEEE colleagues and member groups, online or in person, to build a support group for your profession, industry or project.

The better way to take advantage of the membership is to expand your personal network with colleagues who share your expertise and commitment. Participate in your local IEEE Section or Join IEEE societies or affinity groups focused to your area of specialty or interest, to improve your skills and reinforce our organization.

What can you expect from our Student Branch:



Meetings - As members we are constantly scheduling meetings to share and listen to each other's objectives and shape our experience to meet members expectations.


Ideas - We are always open to hear about new ideas and offer new insights and, when intended, open debates in order to bring support to each other. As a group of partners, we're able to support and help any great idea to come to light.


Workshops - One of the most important activities we offer in the SB is Workshops. They're wanted to fulfill the need of knowledge and experience of the student members and the community. The topics of these workshops can vary from soft skills such as self-management, professional career management, all the way to more technical skills like 3D printing, home automation and programming languages.


Projects - As an IEEE Student Branch, a part of a global organization, we develop projects that help make an impact in our society. We're impacting the world by implementing projects locally.


Step 1 - IEEE Registration

Register on the IEEE official website.
Choose the category “Professional and Education Information” and then select “Instituto Politécnico do Cávado e Ave”.

Membership cost: 27$ (24.30€) annual.

Step 2 - Email Us

Send us an email to ieee.est.sb@ipca.pt containing:

1 - Your full name;
2 - IEEE membership number;
3 - IPCA student ID;

Step 3 - Wait for confirmation

We will check your information and send you an email with the confirmation. All the details will be included on the email and you’ll have access to our room.
